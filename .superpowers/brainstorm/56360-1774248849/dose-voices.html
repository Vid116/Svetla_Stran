Three moments, three voices

Each touchpoint catches the reader in a different mood. The copy should match that moment.

1. HOMEPAGE — The Invitation

Reader is browsing, curious, not yet committed. This is a warm welcome.

"Ena dobra zgodba na dan. Brez klikanja, brez doom-scrollinga."

One good story a day. No clicking, no doom-scrolling.

tvoj@email.si
Naroči se

Tone: Calm, confident. Like a friend recommending something they genuinely enjoy. No urgency.

2. MID-ARTICLE — The Whisper

Reader is mid-story, already engaged, feeling something. Don't break the spell — just plant a seed.

☀️ Všeč? Vsak dan ena taka.

Tone: Minimal. Almost a footnote. Just 4 words + link. No email field here — clicks through to a compact signup. Doesn't interrupt reading flow.

3. END-OF-ARTICLE — The Afterglow

Reader just finished. They felt something. This is the moment of connection — acknowledge what they just experienced.

Dnevna doza dobrega

"Svet ni tak kot ga kažejo. Dokaži si vsak dan."

The world isn't what they show you. Prove it to yourself every day.

tvoj@email.si
Pošlji mi

Tone: Intimate. Philosophical. Speaks to why they came here in the first place — they're tired of bad news. "Pošlji mi" (send me) is personal, not transactional like "Naroči se" (subscribe).

The three moods

Placement Reader's mood Voice CTA word
Homepage Browsing, curious Friendly invitation "Naroči se"
Mid-article Engaged, in-flow Quiet whisper (link, no button)
End-of-article Moved, reflective Intimate manifesto "Pošlji mi"
